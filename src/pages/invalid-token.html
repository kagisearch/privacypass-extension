You send an invalid Privacy Pass token.

This may be for a few reasons, such as:

  • You have not used the extension in a while, and we failed to detect this and make you generate more tokens.
  • You are or were debugging the extension against the staging environment, and have mixed tokens up.
In either case, to keep using Privacy Pass, open the extension settings, and click "Clear state". Then generate new tokens.
